Heat Season 2014, NYC


10/01/2014-Present

October 1st through May 31st is "Heat Season" in New York City

Building owners are required to provide tenants with heat as follows:

  • Between the hours of 6:00 AM and 10:00 PM, if the outside temperature falls below 55 degrees, the inside temperature is required to be at least 68 degrees Fahrenheit;
    &
  • Between the hours of 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM, if the temperature outside falls below 40 degrees, the inside temperature is required to be at least 55 degrees Fahrenheit.
